Retirement Calculator

This calculator estimates how much money you'll need to save to retire based on the "4% rule." The rule suggests that you can safely withdraw 4% of your initial investment each year—adjusted for inflation—without fully depleting your savings.

  • Calculations use inflation-adjusted spending, with an assumed inflation rate of 3.5% per year.
  • For early retirement calculations we assumed your savings at least outperform inflation.
  • No tax is accounted for in this calculator
