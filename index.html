NL - Retirement Calculator

This calculator estimates how much money you'll need to save to retire based on the "4% rule." The rule suggests that you can safely withdraw 4% of your initial investment each year—adjusted for inflation—without fully depleting your savings.

  • Calculations use nominal values (not adjusted for inflation), with an assumed inflation rate of 3.5% per year.
  • For early retirement calculations we assumed your savings at least outperform inflation.
  • No tax is accounted for in this calculator

Required Savings to retire early
To retire early, you’ll need personal savings, as pensions are not paid out before the official retirement age. It is assumed that these savings at least outperform inflation, a annual return of 3.5%. Hence they need to be invested.
